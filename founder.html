About Digital Security Awareness (DSA)

Digital Security Awareness (DSA) is an educational platform dedicated to creating awareness about cybersecurity, cybercrime prevention, digital privacy, artificial intelligence, and the responsible use of technology.

DSA aims to empower individuals, businesses, schools, and communities with the knowledge and skills needed to identify digital threats and protect themselves in an evolving digital world.

Founder: OJ Kgang

Founder of Digital Security Awareness (DSA).

Dedicated to promoting cybersecurity awareness, digital safety, cybercrime prevention, and responsible technology use.

Our Vision

Creating a safer digital world through knowledge by empowering communities with cybersecurity awareness and digital safety skills.

Our Mission

To educate and inspire individuals and organisations to understand cyber risks, protect digital information, and adopt safe online practices.

Our Focus Areas

  • Cyber Fraud Prevention
  • Digital Privacy Protection
  • Artificial Intelligence Awareness
  • Online Safety Education
