Banking Scams

Criminals may use fake calls, messages, or websites to steal banking information. Never share your PIN, password, or verification codes.

WhatsApp and Social Media Scams

Be careful of fake profiles, impersonation, suspicious links, and requests for money or personal information.

Investment Scams

Be cautious of offers promising guaranteed high returns, quick profits, or pressure to invest immediately.

Phishing Attacks

Phishing uses fake emails, websites, and messages to trick people into revealing confidential information.

Identity Theft Prevention

  • Protect your ID information
  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable multi-factor authentication
  • Monitor your accounts regularly

Remember

Stop. Think. Verify before clicking links, opening attachments, or sharing personal information online.
