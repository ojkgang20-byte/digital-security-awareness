🚨 Banking Scams

Criminals use fake calls, SMS messages, emails, and websites to trick victims into revealing banking information.

Protection Tips:

  • Never share your PIN, password, or OTP codes.
  • Do not click suspicious banking links.
  • Verify messages directly with your bank.

📱 WhatsApp & Social Media Scams

Scammers create fake profiles, impersonate people, and send messages requesting money or personal information.

Protection Tips:

  • Verify unexpected requests for money.
  • Do not share personal information with strangers.
  • Enable privacy settings on social media accounts.

💰 Investment & Online Job Scams

Be careful of schemes promising guaranteed profits, quick wealth, or unrealistic employment opportunities.

Warning Signs:

  • Pressure to act immediately.
  • Requests for upfront payments.
  • Promises that sound too good to be true.

🎣 Phishing Attacks

Phishing involves fake emails, messages, or websites designed to steal passwords, financial information, or personal data.

  • Check website addresses carefully.
  • Avoid opening unknown attachments.
  • Report suspicious messages.

🆔 Identity Theft Prevention

  • Protect your identity documents.
  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication.
  • Monitor your accounts regularly.

🛡️ Remember: Stop. Think. Verify.

Before clicking links, opening attachments, or sharing personal information online, take time to verify the request.
