OJ Kgang

Founder & Digital Security Advocate

OJ Kgang is the Founder of Digital Security Awareness (DSA), an initiative dedicated to educating individuals, businesses, schools, and communities about cybersecurity, cybercrime prevention, digital privacy, online safety, and responsible use of technology. His mission is to empower people with practical knowledge that helps them recognise digital threats and stay safe in an increasingly connected world.
